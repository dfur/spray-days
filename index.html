Always confirm with a calibrated weather meter on-site at the time of spraying (wind, Delta T) and follow the product label for specific wind / Delta T / rainfast / inversion conditions. Forecasts cannot reliably detect surface temperature inversions — do a physical check (smoke test, ground vs. 1.5 m temp, mist settling) near dawn and dusk. Mid-winter Delta T is structurally low, so the dominant risks here are inversions and slow leaf-drying, not droplet evaporation.
